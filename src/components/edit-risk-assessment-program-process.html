Standard CDD

Standard customer due diligence procedures that apply to all clients when entering a new business relationship or conducting designated services.

Process Items
Item Responsibility Actions
▶ Verify the identity of all new clients before providing a designated service or establishing a business relationship.
▶ Collect and record the nature and purpose of the business relationship at client onboarding.
Title
Summary
▶ Identify and verify the beneficial owners of all entities (companies, trusts, partnerships) before onboarding.
Title
Summary
▶ Assign an initial ML/TF risk rating to each client at onboarding using the firm's risk assessment methodology.

Enhanced CDD

Enhanced due diligence procedures applied to higher-risk clients, including PEPs, high-risk jurisdictions, and complex structures.

Process Items
Item Responsibility Actions
▶ Obtain senior management approval before establishing a relationship with a high-risk client.
▶ Obtain additional information on the source of funds and source of wealth for high-risk clients.
Title
Summary
▶ Apply enhanced monitoring to high-risk client transactions on an ongoing basis.

Simplified CDD

Reduced CDD measures applicable to clients assessed as presenting a demonstrably lower risk of ML/TF.

Process Items
Item Responsibility Actions
▶ Apply simplified CDD measures to clients that present a demonstrably lower ML/TF risk.
▶ Document the rationale for applying simplified CDD for each applicable client.

Ongoing CDD

Procedures for monitoring client relationships and transactions on a continuing basis to detect changes in risk profile.

Process Items
Item Responsibility Actions
▶ Monitor client transactions on an ongoing basis to ensure consistency with the firm's knowledge of the client.
▶ Update client CDD records whenever there is a material change in client circumstances.

Summary
▶ Conduct periodic reviews of client files to confirm CDD information remains current and accurate.

Procedures for screening clients and associated parties against sanctions lists and registers.

Item Responsibility Actions

Procedures for identifying and reporting threshold transactions (physical currency transfers of AUD 10,000 or more) to AUSTRAC.

Process Items
Item Responsibility Actions

▶ Submit a Threshold Transaction Report (TTR) to AUSTRAC within 10 business days.
